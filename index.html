Durian King Of Fruit's

Known as “the king of fruit,” durian is a large, spiky tropical fruit indigenous to Southeast Asia. Durian has a spiny, thick husk ranging in color from dull green to slightly yellow-brown. Its edible interior is a soft, custard-like pulp that can be white or golden-yellow.
